What is 2S Racing league?

We have some of the most epic on-track battles, strong fields, and great rivalries! We cater for players across a wide range of skill, and if you're looking for more than just racing, such as a full on gaming community, then the 2S Community is the place for you! Check out our discord to sign up or just talk within the community or have a look and some of our live-streamed, commentated races here! also make sure to check out our instagram for weekly updates for our standings and pictures from the races!
